NodeTypes

Starting with document.body, we'll iterate over the child nodes and output them and their nodeType to the console.

Well, since we're appending the node object to a string in our console.log() call, what we're outputting is the output of each node's toString() method. Different browsers will display this differently.

In any case, the output shows each child node of document.body. Note that it does not show the nodes that are the children of the children of the body - those that are deeper in the DOM tree.
